# GEO Content Quality Rubric

Score each dimension from `1` to `5`.

## Dimensions

- `source_quality`: Are the sources current, official, and category-relevant?
- `freshness`: Are time-sensitive claims checked against recent evidence?
- `directness`: Does the deliverable answer the target question early and clearly?
- `fairness`: Are comparisons evidence-based instead of biased promotion?
- `traceability`: Can claims be traced back to cited evidence?
- `anti_hallucination`: Does the output avoid invented facts and fake citations?

## Minimum Passing Interpretation

- No dimension below `3`
- Average score at or above `4`
- `anti_hallucination` must be `5`
